About Sushi Koi

Sushi Koi features Japanese Cuisine made by very experienced sushi chefs who use fresh and high quality ingredients, many of which come from Japan and Asia. Our menu offers wonderful original creations as well as the usual items any good Japanese restaurant offers, including vegetarian options.

Sushi Koi has been a neighborhood fixture in the Forest Hills/Kew Gardens area for many years, the owners being Queens residents themselves, with many of our frequent customers seen as friends.

Our friendly staff looks forward to seeing or hearing from you soon!
